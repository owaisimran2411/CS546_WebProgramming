Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Purchase a Weekly/Bi-Weekly Travel Pass

    Being a totally new location, purchasing a Weekly/Bi-Weekly Unlimited Travel Pass can help save a lot of money in exploring different destination. You can travel freely without the stress of making errors and spending more money!

  2. Mix of Local Currency & US Dollars

    Make sure you have sufficient amount available in local currency for your day to day expenses. Moreover, have some USD on the side as well so that you can convert in case of need. Since most of the countries accept USD payments as well

  3. Pack Seasonal Clothes

    Make sure you check the weather forecast prior to your trip and pack your clothes accordingly
